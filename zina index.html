about us

A company limited by shares in Uganda. Registered and incorporated on the 26th day of February 2021.

ZINAFURIKA SERVICES LIMITED is a company that has built its reputation over the years as the most preferred provider of cleaning services to both commercial and domestic clients. Our strength is in our continuous on-site refresher training, strict management and monitoring systems that keeps our personnel the best executing services at the premises of our clients.

  • We provide highly customised cleaning solutions using environmentally friendly chemicals, state of the art equipment.
  • A well-trained workforce for our clients whenever they need our services.
